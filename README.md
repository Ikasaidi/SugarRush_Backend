# :lollipop: SugarRush :lollipop:


Welcome to **Sugar Rush**, the ultimate city companion for anyone living in **Candy Land**! This app is designed to make urban life sweeter: from managing your train tickets and scanning them with a QR code to checking schedules, staying on top of city news, and even getting the latest weather updates. Sugar Rush takes care of your journey while keeping your personal data safe and secure, all with a touch of fun. No ticket tampering, no sharing with others, and no peeking into anyone else’s info—just smooth, easy, and safe travels through your candy-colored cityscape.
## Main features
**Sugar Rush** is packed with features designed to make city transportation **easy, convenient, and enjoyable** for everyone living in the candy-colored city.  

### User Accounts & Authentication
- **Secure account creation and login** for all users.
- Users are categorized into three types: **Students, Seniors, and Adults**, each with a tailored ticketing system:
  - **Students:** Start with **2 free tickets**, can check their balance, use tickets via QR code, purchase additional tickets at a discounted rate, view train schedules, and receive city news and weather notifications.
  - **Seniors:** Start with **4 free tickets**, can scan tickets via QR code, purchase additional tickets at a reduced price, view train schedules, and get city news and weather updates.
  - **Adults:** Do **not receive free tickets**, but can purchase tickets, validate them with QR codes, check train schedules, and receive notifications.

### Train Schedule Display
- View **train schedules** based on the selected station.
- Schedules are updated automatically depending on the station chosen by the user.

### Train Passage History
- Keep a **history of recent train passages**, including a list of stops and arrival times at each station.

### Notifications System
- Receive **real-time notifications** when the train arrives at a specific stop.
- Get alerts for important events, like **closed railway crossings**.

### Ticketing & QR Pass
- Generate **train tickets as QR codes** for easy validation.
- Scan QR codes to **validate tickets**, with balances automatically updated in the user’s account.
- **Purchase additional tickets** directly through the app.

Sugar Rush keeps commuting **smooth, safe, and a little sweeter** for everyone in the candy-colored city.

## Non-Functional Requirements

To ensure **Sugar Rush** delivers a smooth and reliable experience, the application must meet the following non-functional criteria:

| Criterion          | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **Performance**    | The app must provide **fast response times** when displaying train schedules, tracking trains in real time, and validating tickets via QR code. |
| **Security**       | Personal data must be protected through **secure authentication**, encryption of sensitive information, and compliance with privacy standards. |
| **Scalability**    | The system should support an **increasing number of users** and allow new features to be added without compromising performance. |
| **Usability**      | The interface must be **simple, clear, and intuitive**, making it accessible to all age groups, including students, adults, and seniors. |
| **Compatibility**  | The app must work seamlessly on **major mobile operating systems** (iOS and Android) and be compatible with various devices. |

With these requirements in place, Sugar Rush ensures a **fast, secure, and user-friendly commuting experience** for everyone in the candy-colored city.

## Application Constraints & Technology Stack

For the development of **Sugar Rush**, our team carefully selected a combination of tools and technologies to **streamline the development process** and **maximize efficiency**. Here’s what we chose:

- **Programming Languages:** TypeScript, JavaScript (React Native), Python, MongoDB  
- **Frameworks:** React Native for mobile app development, Node.js with Express for the backend  
- **Data Access:** MongoDB database for **secure storage** of user information and tickets  
- **Data Requests:** REST API using HTTP requests (GET, POST, PUT, DELETE) for communication between the mobile app and the server  

### Key Constraints
- The **user interface** must be simple, clear, and intuitive to ensure usability across **all age groups**.  
- **Security** is a top priority, including secure authentication, protection of sensitive data, and adherence to privacy standards.  
- The project must include **unit and integration tests** to guarantee the **reliability, stability, and performance** of the application.  

With these tools and constraints in place, Sugar Rush aims to deliver a **smooth, safe, and enjoyable experience** for every commuter of Candy Land.

## Installation & Use

Clone the project :

```
git clone https://github.com/ton-compte/SugarRush_Backend.git
cd SugarRush_Backend
```

### Installing Dependencies :

For the frontend :

```
npm install
npm start
```

For the backend, follow the instructions specific to your framework.

Running the application on a device:

- Use an emulator or a physical device to test the application
